KEY PROJECT HIGHLIGHTS

In 2007 and through open competitive bidding, we were awarded our single largest contract worth some USD 30 million by Petronas Methanol Labuan for the engineering, procurement, construction and commissioning assistance of three hot oil heaters for the Labuan Gas Terminal.

This contract entails the engineering design of three new hot oil heaters together with the hot oil expansion and recirculation system. We had the privilege to collaborate with Linde Impianti (for the heater design) and Kencana Bestwide (for the local engineering support and E&I works). Project was completed ahead of schedule and while achieving an overall local content of more than 70%!

This milestone achievement propelled us further as a true integrated service provider.
